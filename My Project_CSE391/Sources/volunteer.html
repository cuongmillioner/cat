Volunteer with Animal Haven

Volunteers are the backbone of our facility and aid our staff in daily operations. While we do have

individuals that prefer to concentrate on one specific area (i.e. cats,mailings), the majority of our

volunteers are multifaceted.



The volunteer experience at the shelter includes

  • dog walking / feeding / handling
  • cat socializing / feeding / handling
  • cleaning animal areas and facility
  • bathing animals
  • greeting guests for special events
  • assisting potential adopters
  • speaking to public about Animal Haven’s mission
